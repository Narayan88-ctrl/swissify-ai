Swissify AI · DayOne Basel

Operational intelligence for biotech workflow complexity.

Swissify AI builds explainable AI workflow coordination software for biotech audit readiness, regulatory operations, and cross-team visibility.

Problem

Biotech teams are slowed by fragmented documents, manual audit preparation, and low workflow visibility.

Solution

An explainable AI workflow layer that maps operational gaps, recommends next actions, and keeps humans in control.

Focus

Audit readiness, regulatory coordination, workflow ownership, and traceable operational reasoning.

Why it matters

Existing systems manage records, but they often do not coordinate operational decisions. Swissify AI helps teams understand who owns what, what is missing, and what needs action before audits or regulatory reviews.

Core platform pillars

Audit readiness

Track missing evidence, unresolved actions, and readiness status.

Workflow coordination

Map tasks, owners, approvals, dependencies, and operational risks.

Explainable AI

Generate traceable recommendations with human review and clear reasoning.

Research direction

How can explainable AI reduce operational complexity and improve audit readiness for biotech teams?
