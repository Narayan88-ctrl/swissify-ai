Problem

Biotech teams are slowed by fragmented documents, manual audit preparation, and low workflow visibility.

Solution

An explainable AI workflow layer that maps operational gaps, recommends next actions, and keeps humans in control.

Focus

Audit readiness, regulatory coordination, workflow ownership, and traceable operational reasoning.

AI

Why current systems fail

Existing systems manage records, but they often do not coordinate operational decisions. Teams still rely on emails, spreadsheets, shared folders, and manual reviews to understand who owns what, what is missing, and what needs action.

What teams have today

  • QMS systems
  • Shared folders
  • Email approvals
  • Spreadsheets

What teams still lack

  • Operational intelligence
  • Workflow ownership
  • Impact analysis
  • Explainable coordination

Core platform pillars

Audit readiness

Track missing evidence, unresolved actions, ownership gaps, and readiness status.

Workflow coordination

Map tasks, owners, approvals, dependencies, and operational risks across teams.

Explainable AI

Generate traceable recommendations with human review and clear reasoning.

Research direction

How can explainable AI reduce operational complexity and improve audit readiness for biotech teams?
